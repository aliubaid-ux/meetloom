Features

Nothing you don't.

Live Video Meetings

Peer-to-peer WebRTC calls with adaptive grid layouts. Invite anyone with a link — no downloads, no accounts.

Instant Screen Recording

Capture your screen, webcam, and microphone simultaneously. One click to start, one click to publish.

AI-Powered Transcripts

Powered by YouTube's world-class ASR engine. Automatic captions and downloadable transcripts — completely free.

In-Room Chat

Real-time messaging during meetings. Share links, notes, and context without interrupting the conversation.

Emoji Reactions

React with 👍 ❤️ 🎉 😂 🔥 without unmuting. Floating animations make calls feel alive and engaging.

Live Screen Sharing

Share your screen with all participants during the meeting with adaptive spotlight layout.

Loom-Style Watch Page

Beautiful video playback with side-by-side transcript. Copy share link, download transcript as .txt.

Password-Protected Rooms

Set a PIN for your meeting room. Only people with the code can join — simple and effective security.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Toggle mic (M), camera (V), chat (C), and more. Power-user friendly controls for seamless flow.
